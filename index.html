The Space

A place to think new ideas & experiment.

Founded and curated with an artistic spirit, Pa Rang Cafe & Art Stay is a peaceful compound located in the heart of historic Chiang Mai. We designed this space to serve as a supportive and comfortable greenhouse for minds to slow down, explore, and create.

Surrounded by foliage, the space transitions seamlessly from an energetic cafe courtyard in the day to a magical, lantern-lit acoustic garden during music nights. It is a nurturing home for travelers, locals, and creative people.

Garden Events & Weekly Workshops

Events are happening frequently in our garden courtyard! We run a highly active and welcoming community space. Check out our signature reoccurring events and dynamic workshops below.

Reoccurring Event
Clay & Hand-building Weekly Sessions

Earthy Terracotta Workshops

Our most popular signature reoccurring workshop. Gather in our quiet garden patio to experiment with authentic local Chiang Mai clay. Learn traditional Lanna hand-building and pinching techniques to mold your own terracotta planters, incense holders, or rustic art pieces under the guidance of our local coordinators.

Happening Frequently
Acoustics & Sound Live Garden Music

Garden Music Nights

A major regular highlight of our community life. Under the glowing paper lanterns, local Chiang Mai singer-songwriters, acoustic instrumentalists, and visiting ambient music practitioners perform sets. A highly popular evening for locals and travelers to share good conversations.

Shared Plates Frequent Suppers

Community Dinners & Suppers

We gather frequently for casual communal meals designed to connect traveling travelers, homestay guests, resident artists, and Chiang Mai's local Thai creative collectives. Share local northern Thai food, fresh drinks, and new collaborative drafts in our comfortable garden nook.

Curatorial & Talk Rolling Events

Artist Talks & Studio Tours

Rolling educational sessions hosted by our global resident artists. Ranging from digital art talks and watercolor illustration to curatorial roundtables and portfolio review circles designed to guide young local artists.

Residency Program

Creative Exchange

We support artistic exploration and slow research by offering a comfortable, inspiring compound in Old Town. Selected artists reside in private rooms and connect with Chiang Mai's local art ecosystem.

💵 Subsidized Rates

To support independent artists, we offer heavily discounted, subsidized rates that simply cover local cleaning, utilities, and room maintenance.

🤝 Community Share

In exchange for subsidized support, resident artists share their creative focus by hosting a Saturday terracotta workshop, a live sound session, or an artist talk open to the public.

  • ⏳ Duration: 1 week to 1 month
  • 🛠️ Resources: Shared garden workspace, studio tools, AC double suite
  • 📋 Selection: Reviewed on a rolling basis; intent-aligned applicants receive pricing logs
