To rank high on Google Maps for terms like **artist cafe**, **artist retreat**, **expat meetup**, **creative coworking**, and **casual meeting space**, use these advanced Google Business Profile settings:

1. **Optimized Business Description (Crucial!):**

A peaceful casual meeting space and artist retreat for digital nomads, writers, and local creatives looking to slow down and connect."*

3. **List Custom Services (Google Maps Services Tab):**
   Google Map rankings are heavily driven by the "Services" list. Add these custom services:

   * `Creative Coworking Space`
   * `Casual Meeting Space`
   * `Expat Meetups & Social Events`
   * `Acoustic Live Music Nights`

   You are allowed to ask and answer questions on your own Google Maps profile. Ask these questions from a personal account (or your business account) and post the answers:

   Post a quick photo update directly on Google Maps once a week. Google indexes these updates immediately for local search queries:
