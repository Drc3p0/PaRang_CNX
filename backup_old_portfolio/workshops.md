# Workshops & Teaching

I design and lead hands-on educational programs focused on creative tech practices, open hardware, and alternative signal transmission. These workshops are structured to allow participants of all technical backgrounds to build functional, interactive systems from scratch.

***

### 🎓 Selected Curriculums

#### **Workshop Title A** (Year)
*   **Duration:** 2-5 Days (Intensive)
*   **Focus:** Custom hardware setups, sensor configurations, and signal output interfaces.
*   **Summary:** Short curriculum summary goes here. Participants learn to wire, program, and map sensor data to high-contrast visual displays.

#### **Workshop Title B** (Year)
*   **Duration:** 1 Week (Studio Seminar)
*   **Focus:** Physical computing, environmental inputs, and real-time mapping systems.
*   **Summary:** Explores how to harness natural ambient fluctuations (light, temperature, noise) to drive generative software engines.

***

### 🤝 Teaching Philosophy

Every workshop focuses on the demystification of technology. By stripping down software and electronics to their core, understandable components, participants are empowered to create their own expressive systems rather than relying on black-box tools.
