This artwork is a dynamic light installation. It represents a physical mapping of electronic signals in a dark gallery context. 

The light output is modulated in real-time according to ambient sonic changes in the room. The viewer's presence triggers variations in density, speed, and contrast.

### Concept and Materials
By projecting light onto suspended optical prisms, the colors separate and bend across the walls, creating an immersive, architectural scale.
* Custom modular micro-controller.
* Suspended custom-cut glass prisms.
* Pure data sonic mapping engine.
